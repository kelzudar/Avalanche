Vision & mission

Our vision is to have a good reputation within the gaming comunity for strong quality products and perfect costumer sevice. In the future we'd also like to co-operate with other publisher such as Riotgames, Epicgames and Sony Entertaintment.
The mission with the product is to give gamers a cheap solution when they buy energy drinks, and at the same time support the game.

Company background story

The company was founded in 2018 by Sebastian, Michelle and Martin. The three of us have always had an interest for video games and that's what inspired us into making the company, which is know as Avalanche. We've always thought that the other energy drink companies had their price value too high and they have never really sponsered or tried to get into the gaming community. We started the co-oporation with Blizzard because we all play games made by Blizzard.
